# RTR-0035
task_id: RTR-0035
title: Bind caches to corpus and contract hashes and remove leaking content
domain: retrieval
problem_type: violation
problem_statement: Cache keys use raw query only and caches persist full retrieval content that can leak prompts or secret-bearing text.
evidence_sources: [actual_truth/contracts/retrieval/determinism_contract.md:104-133, tools/index/search_code.py:48-49,108-111,336-341,487-488]
impacted_files: [tools/index/search_code.py]
current_truth: `query_cache.json` and `query_memory.json` are keyed by raw query and store full content payloads.
emergent_truth: Cache hits can cross contract generations and leak evidence content.
target_state: Cache keys bind to canonical query and manifest hashes, and caches exclude leaking or authority-only content.
verification_method: Changing corpus or contract hash invalidates caches and cache files contain no secret-bearing or prompt content.
dependencies: [RTR-0014, RTR-0013, RTR-0023, RTR-0026, RTR-0029]
launch_impact: critical
