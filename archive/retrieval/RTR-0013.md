# RTR-0013
task_id: RTR-0013
title: Bind authoritative artifacts to contract version
domain: retrieval
problem_type: violation
problem_statement: Authoritative index artifacts do not advertise or validate the required `contract_version`.
evidence_sources: [actual_truth/contracts/retrieval/index_structure_contract.md:247-271, tools/index/build_vector_index.py:32-35, tools/index/search_code.py:32-33]
impacted_files: [tools/index/build_vector_index.py, tools/index/search_code.py]
current_truth: Retrieval and build stages operate without version metadata for authoritative artifacts.
emergent_truth: Contract generations cannot be validated across stored artifacts.
target_state: Every authoritative artifact binds to the same `contract_version` declared by `index_manifest.json`.
verification_method: Retrieval refuses to operate when any authoritative artifact omits or mismatches `contract_version`.
dependencies: [RTR-0007, RTR-0008, RTR-0011]
launch_impact: critical
